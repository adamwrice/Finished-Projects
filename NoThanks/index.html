No Thanks!(giving)

No Thanks! (giving) is a simple game I made exploring behavior trees and boid behavior during a course I was taking about game engine design.

Here is a video demonstrating gameplay!

Music is by bensound

In this game, you play as a turkey soon to be served for dinner by a family of identical humans. However, while their backs were turned, you loudly escaped the oven. The objective of the game is intentially left arbitrary. You can either escape in the shortest time by moving the turkey to the arrows or you can try to avoid the humans for as long as possible.

The boid flocking behavior is simulated using a custom physics engine and the behavior tree is very simple: just wait until something comes into view, if its another human, follow it.

Once the player either gets caught by a human touching them, or safely makes it to the escape arrows, a message appears displaying whether the player won or lost as well as their final time.
